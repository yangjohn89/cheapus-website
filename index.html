About Us

Cheap Us is a furniture store. We provide this sales platform for both buyer and seller. Our customers are mainly from college students. The most important feature of our store is that everything is cheap. We know that college students often move around and they unlikely to buy expensive furniture. So we create this platform for them to sell their unwanted furniture or buy brand new furniture.


For seller, each furniture that show up on this platform has to be checked completely. We make sure the furniture's quality is ready to use, then we put on this platform.
